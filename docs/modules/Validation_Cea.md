## Comparison between all engines

THe different engine available in BG_Flood do treat the reconstruction of water level and depth the cell edges differently and end up treating wet dry front differently That leads to some different results. Kurganov engine deals with a more accurate peak but has deficiencies in the start and end of the "rainfall" whereas the Buttinger reconstruction and the engine 5 (1-single layer multi-layer solver) do better in the rising and falling limb but overshoot the peak.
